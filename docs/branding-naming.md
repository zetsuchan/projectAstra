## Brand Direction A — Editorial Glow
- Visual: soft gradients, airy layouts, serif + elegant sans
- Feel: intelligent, composed, slightly romantic
- Palette: blush, plum, ivory, muted gold
- Motion: slow fades, gentle parallax

## Brand Direction B — Y2K Pop / Chrome
- Visual: glossy UI, bold type, playful icons
- Feel: high energy, cheeky, internet-native
- Palette: hot pink, lilac, silver, electric blue
- Motion: micro-sparkle, quick bounce

## Brand Direction C — Cinematic Twilight
- Visual: dark-to-light gradients, refined serif, minimal chrome
- Feel: intimate, moody, confident
- Palette: midnight blue, mauve, soft amber
- Motion: slow shimmer, light grain
