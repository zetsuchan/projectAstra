# Legal / Privacy Safety Checklist

## Required Disclaimers
- Astrology content is for entertainment and self-reflection only.
- Not medical, legal, or financial advice.

## Age & Content
- If explicit conversation is allowed, add 18+ gate or stricter filters for minors.
- Marketing surfaces remain PG-13.

## Privacy Commitments
- Clear explanation of data usage and storage.
- Opt-in for using diary entries in AI responses.
- User controls for deletion (messages, diary, memory).

## Data Handling
- Encrypt sensitive fields at rest (PlanetScale for Postgres).
- Avoid logging raw chat content.
- Use least-privilege access for internal tooling.

## Consent + Safety
- Avoid coercive or manipulative relationship advice.
- Avoid deterministic claims about health, legal, or financial outcomes.
- Provide supportive guidance when sensitive topics appear.

## Compliance (US-only launch)
- Provide Terms of Service and Privacy Policy before launch.
- App Store privacy labels completed before submission.

## Crypto / Prediction Markets (Later)
- Separate virtual credits vs real-value tokens.
- Legal review required before stablecoin integration.

## Incident Response
- Document response process for data exposure or abuse reports.
- Provide contact email for safety and privacy concerns.
